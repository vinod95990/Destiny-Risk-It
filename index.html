~ HOW TO PLAY ~

  • Ok, the game starts with Player-1
  • So what you have to do is roll the dice and it will generate a number
  • If the number is between [2,6] then your current score will be updated
  • If dice rolls to '1' then it's the turn of other player and your 'Current Score' will become 0.
  • To save your score you have press hold otherwise if dice rolls to '1' then your score will bcome zero
  • When pressed 'HOLD' your score will be saved and control changes to other player
  • If one's 'MAIN-SCORE' i.e. generated by adding your current scores in your turn, reaches 45 or above then that player wins
  • A player can roll dice as many times he wants and current score will get updated accordingly but if dice rolls to '1' then your score will become '0'
